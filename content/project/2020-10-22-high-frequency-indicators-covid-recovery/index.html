Apple Mobility Data

Apple data is determined by index of people seeking directions through the Apple Maps app (driving, transit, walking). This is a 7-day moving average of demand for directions.

As of 2020-10-19 the 7-day moving average for:

  • Driving directions was 127
  • Walking directions was 122
  • Transit directions was 56

The baseline comparison index is set to 100 and is based on the week of January 13, 2020.

Time Spent Away from Home

Google has been tracking the GPS locations of citizens to get a sense of how frequently people are visiting certain locations. Below is a plot a 7-day average of how frequently people have been away of their homes. The data is indexed to Jan 3 - Feb 6, 2020.

As of 2020-10-13, the 7-day average for time Americans spend away from home is -8.0% below the baseline index.

TSA Traffic

This is a 7 day average of the traffic moving through TSA checkpoints throughout the United States. 2020 totals are compared to totals from 2019.

As of 2020-10-19 the 7-day average of travelers moving through TSA checkpoints was 857,906.
