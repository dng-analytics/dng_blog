From this post, an interesting look at how companies are leveraging community in order to foster growth and monetization. The idea of community may have started out simply on social media with straightforward community management, but it’s turned into far more than that, and will continue to grow in importance moving forward.

A lot of this article has to do with future SaaS opportunities, but it’s not too early for brands to start thinking about ways they can efficiently use their communities.
