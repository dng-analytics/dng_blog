An interesting little thought piece arguing that we’re innovating at speed, and churning out work, simply for the sake of doing so. Instead, we need to pause and to really question the how and why of what we’re doing.

The piece highlights 4 different avenues of thought as to how to accomplish this slowing down.

The Beginner’s Mindset which involves seeing things from the perspective of someone who may not know what you’re talking about.

The Question Burst which is a variation on the meeting brainstorm, which focuses on boosting questioning skills.

The Five Whys (no link), which involves asking the question why five times in a row as a way of getting deep into a problem

Negative Framing (no link) which involves admitting that things within a business are far from rosy.
